# Sci-Evo-EPOXY
The name of this dataset is Sci-Evo-EPOXY (Scientific Evolution Dataset for Room-Temperature Curing High-Temperature Resistant Epoxy Adhesives). The dataset comprises 24 pieces of literature, with its scope strictly limited to the field of "epoxy adhesives that cure at room temperature and exhibit high-temperature resistance post-curing." It covers the formulation design of epoxy resins—including the epoxy resin matrix, curing agent systems, and other additives—as well as the effects of variations in epoxy resin components on the curing reaction characteristics, cured structures, mechanical properties, and thermal properties of the epoxy adhesives.
